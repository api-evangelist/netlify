Team Owners can now set a credit limit on AI inference usage to keep Agent Runners and AI Gateway costs within budget. When your team’s usage hits the credit cap you define, active agent runs stop, new agent runs are blocked, and continued AI Gateway usage is paused to help you keep more of your credit balance. This is especially useful for teams actively using AI features who want predictable monthly costs without manually watching the meter.
