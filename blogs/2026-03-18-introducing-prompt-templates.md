To help you and your agents build more effectively, we're introducing reusable Prompt Templates available directly from within Netlify. Browse a curated collection of reusable prompts for common Netlify workflows: accessibility audits, A/B testing with Edge Functions, and more. Each template uses variable placeholders so you can adapt it to your project without starting from scratch.
